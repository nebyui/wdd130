Welcome to Art Building Blocks!

This is a useful reference to help artists learn and serve as a reference for the fundamentals of art. Although each concept may seem simple and elementary, having a mastery of each one is imperative to making masterful art in any medium. All artwork is made using these artistic "building blocks", and master artists simply have great understanding and skill with them, allowing them to create whatever they imagine. Wherever you are on your journey as an artist, be sure to remember these fundamentals. The fundamentals of art can be split into two categories: Principles and Elements.
